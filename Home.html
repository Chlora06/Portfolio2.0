Design Philosophy

I design to solve problems and enhance user experience, prioritizing inclusive to ensure all users have equal access. I focus on identifying and addressing the most critical issues first, often incorporating innovative solutions like AI. Functionality is my core focus, with feedback and iteration playing a vital role in refining the design. I believe in a continuous process of improvement, where testing reveals the real-world impact of the design and helps shape better user experiences.

Design Process

My UX design process is built on iteration and continuous refinement. I start by clearly defining the problem, identifying user pain points, and setting goals. I then move into research, gathering insights through interviews, surveys, and competitor analysis. With these insights, I begin ideation, brainstorming and sketching solutions, and developing user flows. In the wireframing & prototyping phase, I create low and high-fidelity prototypes, keeping functionality and inclusivity at the forefront. Through usability testing and iteration, I gather feedback and refine the design in multiple cycles until it meets both user needs and design objectives. The final design balances usability with aesthetics, and I conclude with reflection, drawing insights that inform my evolving design approach.
